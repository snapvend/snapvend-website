FAQ

The most common questions before teams start

These are the core questions photographers and event teams usually ask first.

Does SnapVend work without internet?

Yes. The main workflow can run over local network or hotspot. Cloud access is not required.

How do clients view the photos?

Clients scan a QR code on the same network, open the browser gallery and make their selections there.

Can I use my own business name and logo?

Yes. Pro plans unlock custom business branding, logo usage and branded delivery.

Which plan removes delivery limits?

Both monthly and yearly Pro plans remove the daily delivery limit.
