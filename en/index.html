Workflow

QR, sessions and delivery in one flow

SnapVend turns direct FTP intake from professional cameras, WiFi or hotspot QR access, session-based client flow and post-delivery reporting into one field-ready operation.

  1. 01

    Import from a pro camera

    Pick photos from local media or transfer frames directly into SnapVend from an FTP-capable professional camera.

  2. 02

    Start WiFi / hotspot QR access

    Bring clients onto the same network without asking them to install the app; the QR connection screen presents the network name, password and access details clearly.

  3. 03

    Open the session gallery with QR

    Each client or event can have its own session gallery, opened in seconds from the browser through a dedicated QR code.

  4. 04

    Show local payment and confirm PAC

    Display ready local payment methods for your business country, review the selection and complete PAC validation.

  5. 05

    Deliver ZIP and review reports

    Deliver approved files as ZIP, then track downloads, sales, conversion and revenue from the in-app reports view without losing the session structure.

FAQ

The most common questions before teams start

These are the core questions photographers and event teams usually ask first.

Does SnapVend work without internet?

Yes. The main workflow can run over local network or hotspot. Cloud access is not required.

Can I transfer directly from a professional camera?

Yes. Photos can move directly into SnapVend from an FTP-capable professional camera over the same network or hotspot.

How do clients view the photos?

Clients first join through a WiFi or hotspot QR connection, then scan the session QR code to open only the gallery created for them in the browser.

What does the session structure do?

Each client, shoot or event can stay in its own session so files, selections, payment flow and delivery records never get mixed, while each outcome stays easier to track.

What can I track in the reports section?

The reports view helps you monitor sales count, revenue, download activity, conversion and average basket without breaking the live field workflow.

Can I use my own business name and logo?

Yes. Pro plans unlock custom business branding, logo usage and branded delivery.

Can I show country-specific payment methods to clients?

Yes. Once the business country is selected, completed local methods appear in the payment popup. Ready local method sets are available for Turkey, Spain, India, China and Japan.

What does the print list do?

The print list keeps selected frames ready for printing without breaking the delivery flow. It gives wedding teams a more controlled way to track which photos should be printed and how print preparation should move forward.

Which languages are supported?

The website, customer gallery and supported flow screens can be presented in these languages: Turkish, English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, Chinese

How does licensing work?

SnapVend licensing is plan based. The free plan covers the core workflow, while Pro plans unlock advanced delivery, branding and operational controls during the active subscription period. Contact us for extra device or team licensing.

Which plan removes delivery limits?

Both monthly and yearly Pro plans remove the daily delivery limit.
